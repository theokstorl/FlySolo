Product Information

The FlySolo, pictured above is the result of tireless work from our dedicated team of engineers. The FlySolo boasts a range of 500 miles on a single charge, and is 100% powered by electricity. It comes fully equipped with our signature fool-proof operating system. Simply push the "start" button, and select the altitute you would like to fly at. Your FlySolo will then automatically ascend to the selected altitude. Point the joystick in the direction you woud like to go; then sit back, relax, and enjoy the flight. Before you know it, you will have reached your destination. Ease off of the joystick, and press the "ground" button. Your FlySolo will comfortably descend, and simply toggle the joystick to find a parking space. Once on the ground, press the "stop" button to turn off and complete your trip.
